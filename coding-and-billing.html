Coding and Billing

Coding

Your practice retains primary coding responsibility, but MPMS reviews complex cases within the standard billing fee and asks for documentation when needed to ensure the appropriate code is used. We keep a curated library of coding resources, including reference materials, association guidance, and government tools.

Posting Methodology Goals

  • Post clean charges/claims within 24 hours of receiving full posting information.
  • Alert clients about non-clean charges/claims within 48 hours.
  • Verify every charge for accuracy before submission to payers or patients.
  • Post payments within 24 hours once all posting fields are complete.
  • Submit insurance claims every business day.
  • Send statements on a schedule that aligns with your practice.

Reporting Methodology

We partner to define the reports you need from your practice management system, aiming to close each month within three business days once your team shares final data. Month-end packages arrive within 15 business days and include monthly accounts receivable graphs, aging analysis, collection ratios versus MGMA benchmarks, and optional customized reports by provider/location.

Review of Encounters

  • Annual E&M encounter reviews are available, with additional reviews offered at a competitive rate.
  • Documented encounters can be audited for coding accuracy and completeness per request for an additional fee.
  • Special reviews tied to compliance efforts such as RAC audits are provided at no extra charge when needed.

Other Management Analysis

MPMS can perform broader management analysis of your revenue cycle and billing practice profiles. Depending on the project scope, mutually agreed fees or consulting rates may apply for those expanded services.
