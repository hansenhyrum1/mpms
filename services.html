Monthly Fees

The cost for basic billing services is included in the monthly billing fee, which is a percent of the money collected minus refunds. MPMS can vary billing fee rates or add services depending on the support you need.

  • Statement costs and postage
  • Software licenses
  • Clearinghouse costs
  • Software training for billing, scheduling, and reception
  • Connectivity to practice management systems
  • Contracting and payer negotiation services
  • Credentialing services
  • Coding services for encounters

Compliance and Confidentiality

  • Compliance services are part of the monthly billing fee.
  • All MPMS services meet HIPAA privacy and security regulations.
  • We can provide a copy of our billing and coding compliance plan upon request.
  • MPMS addresses suspected compliance issues with appropriate urgency.
  • Compliance requires collaboration between MPMS and your office.
  • At the onset, MPMS and each client sign a Business Associate Agreement.

How to Get Started

  • Provide accounts receivable data so MPMS can determine the monthly billing rate.
  • Share encounter counts, charges, payments, and adjustments from the past 12 months.
  • Supply payer mix data, including Medicare, Medicaid, commercial, self-pay, and any other plans, plus aging and credit balance analyses.
  • Tell us the status of your accounts receivable and major issues facing your practice.
  • Discuss any additional services you'd like beyond AR work.
  • Once the billing fee and add-ons are set, MPMS prepares a Billing Service Agreement.
  • Services typically begin within 30 days of a signed agreement—often sooner when timelines permit.
